# Credit Card Financial Dashboard

## Project Objective
Developed a comprehensive weekly credit card dashboard that provides real-time insights into key performance metrics and customer trends, enabling stakeholders to monitor and analyze operations effectively.

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
* **Total Revenue:** 55.3M
* **Total Interest:** 7.8M
* **Total Transaction Amount:** 44.5M
* **Total Transaction Count:** 655.7K

---

## Dashboard Insights

### 1. Customer Demographics
* **Age Group:** The 40-50 age group is the highest revenue contributor, generating approximately 25M.
* **Education:** Graduate-level customers lead in revenue contribution, accounting for roughly 22.3M.
* **Marital Status:** Married customers contribute 51% of total revenue, while Single customers account for 42%.
* **Income Group:** High and Very High income groups drive the majority of the total revenue.

### 2. Transaction & Card Analysis
* **Card Category:** The Blue card is the primary driver of the portfolio, accounting for 46.1M in revenue.
* **Transaction Method:** Swipe transactions are the most frequent method used by customers.
* **Expenditure Type:** Bills represent the highest expense category, followed by Entertainment and Fuel.
* **Quarterly Trend:** Revenue remained stable throughout the year, with a peak performance in Q3.
