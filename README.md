# Credit Card Financial Dashboard

## Project Objective
To develop a comprehensive weekly credit card dashboard that provides real-time insights into key performance metrics and customer trends, enabling stakeholders to monitor and analyze operations effectively.

## Dashboard Insights

### 1. Customer Demographics
* [cite_start]**Age Group:** The 40-50 age group is the highest revenue contributor, generating approximately 25M[cite: 91, 92, 98].
* [cite_start]**Education:** Graduates lead in revenue contribution, accounting for roughly 22M[cite: 42, 44, 145].
* [cite_start]**Marital Status:** Married customers contribute 51% of total revenue, while Single customers account for 42%[cite: 54, 61].
* [cite_start]**Income Group:** High and Very High income groups drive the majority of the revenue[cite: 68, 71].

### 2. Transaction & Card Analysis
* [cite_start]**Card Category:** The **Blue** card is the most popular, driving 46M in revenue[cite: 136, 166].
* [cite_start]**Transaction Method:** **Swipe** transactions are the most frequent, followed by Chip and Online[cite: 179, 182, 186].
* [cite_start]**Expenditure Type:** **Bills** represent the highest expense category, followed by Entertainment and Fuel[cite: 178, 180, 181].
* [cite_start]**Quarterly Trend:** Revenue remained relatively stable across Q1, Q2, and Q3, with a slight dip in Q4[cite: 140, 141, 142, 143].
