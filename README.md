# Credit_Card_Finance_DashboardCredit Card Financial DashboardProject OverviewThis project involves the development of a comprehensive Credit Card Weekly Dashboard that provides real-time insights into key performance metrics. The goal is to enable stakeholders to monitor financial health, track transaction trends, and analyze customer demographics effectively.Key Insights (from Dashboard)Total Revenue: $55.3M Total Interest Earned: $7.8M Total Transaction Amount: $44.5M Customer Demographics: The 40-50 age group contributes the highest revenue, totaling approximately $25M.Card Performance: The Blue card category is the primary driver of revenue, accounting for over $46M in transaction value.Transaction Trends: Swipe is the most preferred method of transaction, significantly outperforming Chip and Online methods.Top Expense Categories: Revenue is primarily driven by Bills, followed by Entertainment and Fuel.Tech StackData Visualization: Power BI DesktopDatabase: SQL Server / PostgreSQL (for data storage and extraction)Data Processing: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for calculated measuresProgramming: Python (for data cleaning and preprocessing)Dashboard Features1. Customer ReportRevenue Analysis: Segmented by age, gender, education level, and income group.Geographic Distribution: Revenue breakdown across major states including TX, NY, CA, and FL.Marital & Social Status: Analysis of revenue based on marital status and home ownership.2. Transaction ReportQuarterly Performance: A line-and-cluster chart showing revenue and transaction counts across Q1–Q4.Occupational Insights: Revenue tracking across different professional sectors like Business, White-collar, and Govt.Expenditure Type: Detailed view of spending habits (Grocery, Food, Travel, etc.)
